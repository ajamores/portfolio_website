How AA Batteries Work:

1. Chemical Reaction at Anode (-): Zinc releases electrons and positive zinc ions.

2. Electron Flow: Electrons travel through the wire (creating electricity) to power devices.

3. Ion Flow: Positive ions travel through the electrolyte.

4. Reaction at Cathode (+): Electrons combine with manganese dioxide and positive ions.

Key Insight: The circuit must be complete! Electrons flow through the wire, ions flow through the electrolyte.
